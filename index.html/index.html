User Research

We conducted a total of six interviews in order to discover what reading habits people had and how they went about finding books of interest. We then surveyed a small group of avid readers ages 26 through 44 on their reading habits and how to search for books. With the interview and survey data we learned that users usually rely on recommendations and research through search engines.

Problem statement

Our app Book Bind was designed to provide users with the ability to stream books relating to recommended interests or careers. We have observed that users are mindful of spending time reading books that don't pertain to what they are searching for. How might we provide book recommendations without making the user feel their time has been wasted?
